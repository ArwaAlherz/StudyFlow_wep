StudyFlow
StudyFlow is a smart study planner web application designed to help students organize their academic life more effectively.
The platform provides a simple and user-friendly interface where students can manage their study tasks, subjects, schedules, and track their progress.

Project Description
StudyFlow was developed to solve a common problem faced by many students: difficulty in organizing study tasks and managing time effectively.
This web application helps students plan their study activities in one place, making it easier to stay organized, focused, and productive.

Features
The StudyFlow web application includes several useful features:

Dashboard
Displays an overview of tasks, study hours, and completion rate.

Subjects Management
Allows students to organize their courses and track study materials.

Task Management
Students can add, edit, and manage their study tasks.

Calendar
Helps students schedule assignments, exams, and study sessions.

Progress Tracking
Shows the student's learning progress across different subjects.

Notes Section
A place for students to write important study notes.

Dark Mode
Provides a comfortable interface for studying at night.
Benefits for Students

StudyFlow helps students:
Organize their academic tasks in one place.
Manage their study time more effectively.
Track their learning progress.
Stay motivated by visualizing achievements.
Reduce stress by planning their schedule clearly.
